Differences Between H.264/AVC and H.265/HEVC

  1. In contrast to H.264/AVC, motion compensation with bi-prediction is allowed in both existing profiles which include inter prediction, i.e. Main and Main 10. [1].
  2. The concept of Tier, which is used with Profile and Level to specify the variation of capabilities of the standard, is new in HEVC compared with AVC [1].
